Under the Hood

Built on Cutting-Edge Technology

JeeThy Labs harnesses the latest in AI infrastructure, leveraging Google Cloud's powerful ecosystem to deliver fast, reliable, and scalable AI experiences.

Google Cloud Infrastructure

Our entire platform runs on Google Cloud infrastructure — utilizing Vertex AI, Gemini, Lyria, Cloud Run, and Cloud Storage for elastic scaling, zero-downtime deployments, and global edge performance.

Large Language Models

Integrated with state-of-the-art LLMs through Google's Gemini API, OpenAI-compatible endpoints, and custom fine-tuned models optimized for Southeast Asian languages including Khmer.

Enterprise-Grade Security

AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. OAuth 2.0 authentication, rate limiting, abuse detection, and GDPR-compliant data handling keep your data safe at all times.

Real-Time Processing

Responses stream in real-time via WebSocket and SSE protocols. Average AI response latency under 200ms thanks to model caching, GPU acceleration, and intelligent request routing.

Open API & Webhooks

REST and GraphQL APIs allow seamless integration into your existing workflows. Webhook support for async tasks, rate limit controls, and SDK libraries in Python, JavaScript, and Go.

Deployed on Railway Pro

Backend services are deployed on Railway Pro with auto-scaling, persistent volumes, private networking, and GitHub CI/CD integration — ensuring 99.9% uptime and instant rollbacks.

Our Story

About JeeThy Labs

Our Mission

JeeThy Labs was founded with one clear mission: to democratize access to powerful AI tools for creators, students, entrepreneurs, and businesses across Southeast Asia and beyond. We believe AI should be accessible, affordable, and useful — not just for tech giants, but for everyone.

Born in Cambodia and powered by Google Cloud, our platform bridges the gap between cutting-edge AI capabilities and real-world practical applications. From helping a student write a better essay to empowering a small business owner create marketing content, JeeThy Labs is the AI companion for the modern creator.

Our Vision

To become the leading AI tools platform in Southeast Asia — a hub where innovation meets accessibility, where language is no barrier, and where every user — from Phnom Penh to Singapore — can harness the power of AI to build something extraordinary.
